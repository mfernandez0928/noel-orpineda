🚀 Quantum Family Landing Page
Digital Onboarding & Lead Management System for Noel Orpineda

✨ Key Features
Modern Hero Section: Malinaw na Value Proposition at Call-to-Action.

Member Reviews Feed: Styled na parang "Mighty Networks" community feed para sa instant social proof.

Facebook Live Integration: Link sa public wall ni Coach Noel para sa real-time updates.

Multi-Step Onboarding Modal:

Step 1: Broker Selection (VT Markets / PU Prime) na may referral codes.

Step 2: Data Collection Form (Name, Email, Broker, Capital).

Step 3: Access Granted screen na may direct links sa GC at Training Platforms.

Automated Database: Konektado sa Google Sheets via Google Apps Script (100% Free).

Email Alerts: Instant email notification kay Coach tuwing may bagong "Initiate Protocol" submission.

Floating Messenger Button: Para sa mabilis na inquiry direkta sa inbox ni Coach.

🛠️ Tech Stack
Frontend: HTML5, Tailwind CSS (via CDN), Vanilla JavaScript.

Backend/Database: Google Apps Script + Google Sheets.

Icons: FontAwesome.
